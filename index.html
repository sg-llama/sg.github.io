About

Delivery-focused. Domain-curious.

I work best in environments where the problem is real, the stakeholders are senior, and the path forward isn't yet obvious. I've led products in heavily regulated settings — GDPR, HIPAA, RICS — and across teams ranging from global enterprises to early-stage startups.

I bring the same rigour to product strategy as to day-to-day delivery: from roadmap sessions with leadership to running discovery, research, experimentation, and QA.

While I don't come from an engineering background, I've spent much of my career on technical products, partnering closely with engineers to translate complex requirements into practical solutions.
